Our Story & Trust

Vetham Kuzhumam

Vetham Kuzhumam Spiritual Trust has its roots springing to the movement started by the great Yogi, Shree Vethathri Maharishi. Shri Vethathiri Maharishi was born in 1911 in the village of Guduvancheri, 30km south of Chennai, India, into an indigent weaver’s family. Yogiraj Vethathiri Maharishi, established WCSC in the year 1958. The Vetham trust was later formed in the year 2017 with the motto to continue to fulfil the aim of Vethathri Maharishi “World Peace through Individual Peace
